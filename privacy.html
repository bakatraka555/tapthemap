Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

At TapTheMap, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address (optional): For transaction receipts and captain dashboard access
  • Captain Information: Handle, social media links, bio, payment details (for captains only)
  • Payment Information: Processed and stored by Stripe/PayPal (we do not store card details)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, interactions with the platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address
  • Analytics: Google Analytics data (anonymized)
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for platform functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Processing transactions and sending receipts
  • Managing captain accounts and processing payouts
  • Improving platform functionality and user experience
  • Analyzing platform usage and statistics
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Communicating important updates (captains only)

3. Payment Data

All payment transactions are processed through Stripe or PayPal. We do not store your credit card information or bank account details.

  • Stripe: Processes and stores payment information securely (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • PayPal: Processes payments through their secure platform
  • We only receive transaction confirmation and basic transaction details
  • For more information, review Stripe's Privacy Policy and PayPal's Privacy Policy

4. Captain Data

Captains provide additional information for account management and payouts:

  • Contact Information: Email address for dashboard access and communications
  • Public Profile: Handle, bio, social media links (publicly visible)
  • Payment Details: Stripe account, PayPal email, or IBAN (securely stored)
  • Earnings Data: Commission calculations and payout history

Public vs. Private Data:

  • Public: Handle, bio, social links, total hearts generated, leaderboard position
  • Private: Email, exact earnings, payment details, analytics

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal (for transaction processing)
  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics (anonymized data)
  • Hosting Providers: Netlify, Supabase (for platform operation)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure database with encryption at rest (Supabase)
  • No storage of payment card information
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication for captain dashboards

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics (can be disabled in browser)
  • Referral Tracking: To attribute transactions to captains

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

8. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under GDPR and EU privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@tapthemap.world

9. Data Retention

We retain data as follows:

  • Transaction Data: 7 years (legal requirement for financial records)
  • Captain Accounts: Duration of active membership + 1 year
  • Analytics Data: 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Inactive Accounts: Deleted after 2 years of inactivity

10. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites (e.g., captain social media profiles). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies.

11. Children's Privacy

TapTheMap is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of data collected from children, we will delete it promptly.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EU. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Stripe and PayPal's GDPR-compliant data processing agreements
  • Netlify and Supabase's EU data centers and compliance certifications

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@tapthemap.world
  • General Inquiries: info@tapthemap.world
