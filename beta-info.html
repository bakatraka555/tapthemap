Frequently Asked Questions

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe or PayPal, industry-leading payment processors with bank-level security.

Are there transaction limits?

During early access, we have monthly volume limits to ensure platform stability. These limits will be removed at full launch.

When will captains receive payouts?

Payouts are processed monthly during the first week of each month. During early access, payouts are manually reviewed for security.

What changes at full launch?

Automated payouts, unlimited transaction volume, additional features, and expanded payment options.

Will my captain status change?

No. Early access captains keep their status and may receive additional benefits at full launch.
